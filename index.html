About Us

Why choose our furniture?

Ideku is a leading furniture company with a rich history since its establishment in 2017 in Tangerang. We specialize in making high-quality and stylish furniture that enhances your living space. Ideku, which started as a small company in 2017 with only 7 employees, has grown into a furniture brand that transforms homes with stylish designs. Ideku also collaborates with local furniture craftsmen to produce furniture products with the best quality and as a form of our concern by helping local craftsmen market their products online.
